Our Journey

The story of BNG Gas Station began in a very humble way. In its early days, the business started simply by selling fuel in small quantities using bottles and containers. Despite the limited resources, the founders showed determination, hard work, and a strong commitment to serving their customers. Through the years, their persistence paid off as the business gradually grew and earned the trust of the community.

Because of their dedication and continuous effort, what once started as a small-scale operation eventually turned into a successful venture. This success made it possible for them to build their own full-service gasoline station, now proudly known as BNG Gas Station. Today, it stands as a symbol of their journey from simple beginnings to a more established and reliable fuel provider.

BNG Gas Station has now been operating for 28 years, a testament to its stability and strong foundation in the industry. Behind this success are the hardworking owners, the married couple Girlie Canilao and Enrico Canilao, whose vision and perseverance played a key role in transforming their small business into what it is today.

Our Vision

To be a trusted and leading fuel provider in the Philippines, delivering reliable energy solutions that power progress and everyday mobility.

Our Purpose

We are committed to supporting everyday journeys by providing safe, efficient, and high-quality fuel products that meet the needs of our customers and communities.

Towards this end, we commit to:

  • Optimizing the use of our resources for sustainable growth
  • Delivering high-quality fuel that meets industry standards
  • Ensuring exceptional customer satisfaction at every touchpoint
  • Building strong and lasting partnerships
  • Promoting environmental responsibility in our operations
  • Fostering an inclusive and supportive workplace

For 28 years, our company has been a trusted partner in driving progress—continuously evolving, supporting community development, and improving the lives of countless Filipinos.
